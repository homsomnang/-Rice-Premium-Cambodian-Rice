Our Story

Rooted in Cambodian Tradition

Nestled along the banks of the Mekong River, our family farm has cultivated the finest rice varieties for over a decade. We combine ancient Khmer farming wisdom with modern quality standards to deliver rice that nourishes bodies and honors our heritage.

🌱

100% Natural Farming

No artificial pesticides. We rely on traditional water-management and organic compost.

🔬

Quality Tested

Every batch is lab-tested for purity, moisture content, and grain integrity before export.

🌍

Global Export Ready

Certified for export to the EU, USA, China, and Southeast Asia. Full documentation provided.

Our Products

Discover Our Rice Varieties

Each variety is harvested at peak season to ensure maximum aroma, texture, and nutritional value.

🌸
Jasmine Rice
📍 Battambang Province

Long-grain, naturally fragrant rice with a soft floral aroma. Perfect for steaming and Asian cuisine. Our most popular export variety.

$30 / metric ton
⬜
White Long-Grain Rice
📍 Battambang Province

Clean, neutral-flavored long-grain white rice. Ideal for restaurants, hotels, and large-scale food production. Available in 5% broken grade.

$30 / metric ton
⭐
Glutinous Sticky Rice
📍 Battambang Province

Traditional Khmer sticky rice with high starch content. Essential for Cambodian desserts, Laotian cuisine, and Thai cooking. Tender and chewy.

$25 / metric ton
🔴
Red Cargo Rice
📍 Battambang Province

Nutrient-rich whole-grain red rice with a nutty flavor and firm texture. High in fiber, antioxidants, and naturally gluten-free. Excellent for health markets.

$25 / metric ton
