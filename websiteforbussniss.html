Our Story

Rooted in Cambodian Tradition

Nestled along the banks of the Mekong River, our family farm has cultivated the finest rice varieties for over a decade. We combine ancient Khmer farming wisdom with modern quality standards to deliver rice that nourishes bodies and honors our heritage.

🌱

100% Natural Farming

No artificial pesticides. We rely on traditional water-management and organic compost.

🔬

Quality Tested

Every batch is lab-tested for purity, moisture content, and grain integrity before export.

🌍

Global Export Ready

Certified for export to the EU, USA, China, and Southeast Asia. Full documentation provided.
